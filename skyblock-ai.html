Skyblock AI

Skyblock AI is a bot that tries to make your Skyblock experience as good as possible.
You can check the auction and bazaar prices or view the bazaar price history to maximize your profit.
You can check your progress, have your networth calculated or get a hint of what you might do if you are new to the game.
Skyblock AI also has a timer command so you know when the next events are coming up.

Skyblock AI is constantly being improved and has a steadily growing community.
